Happy Holiday Ham Recipe

This is my families all time favorite dish when it comes to any holiday. Is is honeybaked or homebaked... the table will never know!

  • 6-10lb Ham
  • 1/4 cup whole cloves
  • 1/4 cup dark corn syrup
  • 2 cups honey
  • 2/3 cup butter
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Score ham and stud with whole cloves. Place ham in foil lined pan
  3. Over a medium high heat stovetop, heat the dark corn syrup, honey and butter for the glaze
  4. Brush the glaze over the ham and baked for 1 hour and 15 minure in the oven and baste every 10 to 15 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
